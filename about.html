about cloud labs

Cloud labs was established in 2013 in Budapest, Hungary, by a group of experienced digital security specialists. At the late 2013, the first official market version of Ekran Systems was released. The company, supported by private investors in Hungary, focuses on the development of Ekran System product and delivering it to the customers worldwide via its global partner network. We see our mission in providing out customers with an efficient and easy-to-use tool to answer all their insider threat monitoring needs as well as improve their compliance and meet security audit requirements. Since its inception in 2013, Cloud Labs continuously grows engaging customers in both SMB and big enterprise segments working with small firms, educational institutions, and Fortune 500 companies.
